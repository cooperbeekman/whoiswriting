Layers

June 21, 2024

​

​

The objective of all organic matter, and in this case, specifically humans is "full expression"

Just as a tree grows to its peak height or a lion to its largest size

The fundamental and most simplest point being reached is "full expression"

For humans, full expression is when we are living life to its absolute pinnacle

Full expression of personality, curiosity, fulfillment and love

Humans can only reach full expression when we activate and understand the goal striving mechanism within us

And you can only active this mechanism once you remove all your layers

Most people dont know who they are

They dont know why they like what they like

They dont know why they do what they do

They have no true sense of who they are

And with this comes immense internal conflict that gets worse and worse as they pretend to be somebody/something they are not

The online money space is a prime example

A young man that realizes the world is a lot bigger than going to school and getting a 100k-500k per year job

Now finds himself in a state of lack of confidence and shame as he sees people his age crushing life, making tons of money, speaking in a certain way, liking a certain set of things

Since this young man has spent the last 5-10 years building no moat for himself, no proprietary thoughts, set of beliefs, and parameters

He internally latches onto exterior personality and archetypes to protect his ever growing and unidentifiable lack on confidence and individualism

And with this, he starts adding layers. More and more layers get added on, and the distance between the true self and the life being expressed widens and widens. These layers are like masks, shielding his true self from the world, but also trapping him in a cycle of pretending and performing. He’s lost in a maze of expectations and comparisons, far from his authentic self.

​
These layers block you from reaching full expression. They cloud your mind and distort your true desires, making it impossible to activate your success mechanism. You see, the success mechanism inside you is designed to work with your true self, your real goals and passions. When you’re not living as your true self, the mechanism can’t function properly. It’s like trying to navigate with a broken compass.

​
Think about it. If you’re constantly pretending to be someone you’re not, you’re feeding your mind false information. Your thoughts, beliefs, and interpretations are all based on a facade. This means your creative mechanism is working towards goals that aren’t truly yours. It’s striving for success based on an identity that doesn’t belong to you. No wonder it feels like you’re spinning your wheels and getting nowhere.

To activate your success mechanism, you need to strip away these layers. You need to reconnect with who you truly are. This means facing yourself honestly and letting go of the need to impress others or fit into a certain mold. It’s about finding your own path, your own voice, and your own dreams.

Start by asking yourself some deep questions: Who am I really? What do I genuinely enjoy? What are my true strengths and passions? Why do I do the things I do? Reflect on these questions without judgment. Be brutally honest with yourself.

Once you begin to peel back the layers, you’ll start to see the real you. This process can be uncomfortable and even scary, but it’s necessary for your growth. As you uncover your true self, you’ll find that your goals become clearer, your actions more aligned, and your confidence starts to build naturally. Your success mechanism will kick in, guiding you towards goals that resonate with your true self.

Reaching full expression isn’t about perfection. It’s about authenticity. It’s about living in alignment with who you truly are. When you do this, you’ll find that life flows more smoothly. Opportunities will arise, and success will feel more achievable because you’re no longer fighting against yourself.
